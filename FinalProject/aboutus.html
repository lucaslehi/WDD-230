Who We Are

Bountiful Foods originated in 1985 when two close friends, Emily and David, decided to venture into the world of healthy and organic food. Emily, a devoted food enthusiast, and David, a skilled chef, shared a common vision for offering affordable, top-notch organic food choices. Recognizing a gap in the market, they embarked on their journey with a modest storefront in Los Angeles. There, they introduced locally-sourced produce, meats, and dairy products. Over time, their dedication to quality and exceptional customer service led to the store's burgeoning reputation. They expanded their offerings to include prepared foods and catering services. In the early 1990s, Bountiful Foods expanded further and opened a beloved second location in Carlsbad. The community warmly embraced this new establishment. The success of the Carlsbad location motivated Emily and David to take their brand to greater heights. Thus, they opened several more locations throughout Southern California. Today, Bountiful Foods stands as a prominent name in the organic food industry, boasting a total of 10 locations in the region. Despite their growth, Emily and David remain true to their values. Sustainability, ethical sourcing, and active community involvement continue to be central to their mission. Their unwavering commitment to providing wholesome and delectable food options has earned them a devoted and loyal customer base.
