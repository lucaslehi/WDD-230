Who We Are

Bountiful Foods was founded in 1985 by two friends, Emily and David. Emily was a food enthusiast and David was an experienced chef. They shared a passion for healthy and organic food, and they noticed a gap in the market for affordable, high-quality organic food options. Starting with just a small storefront in Los Angeles, they began to offer locally-sourced produce, meats, and dairy products. Over time, the store gained a reputation for its commitment to quality and customer service, and they began to expand their offerings to include prepared foods and catering services. In the early 1990s, Bountiful Foods opened a second location in Carlsbad, which quickly became a favorite among the local community. With the success of their second store, Emily and David decided to expand their reach further, opening additional locations throughout Southern California. Today, Bountiful Foods is a well-known name in the organic food industry, with a total of 10 locations in the region. They continue to prioritize sustainability, ethical sourcing, and community involvement, and their commitment to providing healthy, delicious food options has earned them a loyal customer base.
