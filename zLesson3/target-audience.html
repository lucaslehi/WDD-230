Brasilia's Chamber of Commerce

Target Audience

The Brasilia's Chamber of Commerce website is targetting local businesses and companies within the Brasilia area, who are looking to grow and develop their organizations.

  • Business Owners: This persona is the primary target audience for our commerce website. They can be new or old business owners - including small or big organizations, those that are looking to network with other local business and access and support their services.
  • Prospective Business Owners: This persona represent individuals who are interested in starting their own business or bringing new business to the area.
  • Local Residents: This persona represents individual who live in the area and are interested in supporting local business.

Scenarios

The reason why someone would access our chamber's website could include some of the following:

  • To find local events and activities organized by the Chamber, such as festivals, concerts, and comunity events.
  • To access a directory of other business in the area and network with potential partners or clients.
  • To stay up to date with the latest news and trends in the local. business community
  • To access job postings and career resources offered by local. businesses
  • To access training and certification programs offered by the Chamber or other businesses in the area.
